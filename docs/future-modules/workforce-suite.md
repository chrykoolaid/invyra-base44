# Invyra Workforce Suite — Deferred Future Scope

Payroll & Rostering and Time Tracking are deferred future Invyra Workforce Suite concepts.

They are not part of the current Invyra Inventory Roadmap and should not appear in the active Inventory sidebar until a separate workforce scope is approved.

## Current boundary

- Do not expose Payroll & Rostering as an active Inventory module.
- Do not expose Time Tracking as an active Inventory module.
- Do not add live payroll execution, wage processing, tax handling, or compliance execution inside the Inventory build.
- Do not add active punch capture or attendance tables inside the Inventory build.
- Do not merge workforce planning into Inventory Settings.

## Future product direction

These concepts may return later as a separate Invyra Workforce Suite after the Inventory platform is stable. At that time, Payroll & Rostering should represent planned labour, while Time Tracking should represent actual worked-time evidence and manager-reviewed corrections.
